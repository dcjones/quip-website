Quip

About

Quip compresses next-generation sequencing data in the FASTQ and SAM/BAM formats with extreme prejudice. It is lightweight, dependency-free, and open source.

Quip is distributed as pristine ANSI C99 source code, compatible with any vaguely Unix-like operating system.
